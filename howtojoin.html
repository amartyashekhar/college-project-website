Registration Guide

Getting started with Happie Ride is simple. Choose your portal below to see the step-by-step registration process tailored for you.

For Parents

Secure a premium, safe, and reliable scooter commute for your child:

  1. 1
    Fill Out the Details: Click the registration button below to access our dedicated parent onboarding form sheet.
  2. 2
    Submit Routing Info: Provide your contact details, student credentials, and precise school address parameters.
  3. 3
    Schedule Confirmation: Our logistics backend processes your route and contacts you via WhatsApp to finalize pickup timing.

For Riders

Join our professional network, secure optimized routes, and earn consistently:

  1. 1
    Online Application: Open our official driver application form link hosted directly through Google Forms.
  2. 2
    Background Verification: Complete mandatory identity screening, license checks, and basic vehicle safety evaluations.
  3. 3
    Onboarding & Training: Undergo rapid roadmap training on speed management and start picking up student routes.
