about us

The Problem

Everyday school commutes are a constant source of anxiety for parents. Relying on unverified local transport options often means dealing with unpredictable delays, lack of accountability, and zero visibility into where your child is during transit.

Our Solution

Happie Ride bridges this gap by offering a structured, dependable monthly transport ecosystem. We combine thoroughly vetted professional riders with strict safety protocols to ensure that a student’s journey to the classroom is secure, punctual, and entirely stress-free.

Our Aim

We aim to redefine student transit by making premium, safe, and joyful commutes accessible to every family. We aren't just moving students—we are protecting a parent's peace of mind every single morning.
